Leaders in building materials

Established in 2013, the Brohks Group started it's venture in Lusaka, Zambia
with trading of structural steel, roofing sheets, hardware and cooking pots. The group has
diversified into manufacturing of PVC Pipes with the establishment of Brohks Enterprises Ltd in 2017.
It has also promoted Green Energy in Zambia through their projects on Bio Energy.
